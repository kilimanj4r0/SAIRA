Vlad Vinogradov, co-founder and CTO of Optic, has been included in Forbes' "30 Under 30" long list in the Science and Technology category. Optic, a joint startup focused on detecting NFT forgeries, was founded by Vlad, a graduate of an IT university, along with former Google Product Director Andrey Doronichev and EORA founder Roman Doronin. The company has attracted $11 million in funding from a Google investor. Vlad also co-founded EORA, a startup studio that develops machine learning-based computer vision solutions and oversees the development of various open-source projects.
